Ver grafica X,X',X'... vs Y con labels

      x = ["A","B","C","D","E"]
      y2=[5,5,5,7,6]
      y3=[1,1,9,9,0]
      y = [3,5,4,2,6]
      
      plt.plot(x, y, label="y", color="blue")
      plt.plot(x, y2, label="y1", color="black")
      plt.plot(x, y3, label="y3", color="red")
      plt.legend()
      plt.show()
